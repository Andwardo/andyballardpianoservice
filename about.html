About Andy Ballard Piano Service

With over two decades of experience, Andy Ballard Piano Service provides trusted, professional piano tuning, repair, and restoration across Dallas, Collin, Denton, Tarrant, Johnson, Ellis, and Rockwall counties in Texas. Andy's philosophy is simple: deliver precise, reliable service with the care and attention your instrument deserves.

Decades of Expertise

After years spent mastering the fine art of piano service, Andy developed a reputation not only for outstanding traditional tuning and repair but also for specialized work with player piano systems. Whether your piano needs standard maintenance or advanced troubleshooting for systems like PianoDisc, QRS Pianomation, Bösendorfer CEUS or Yamaha Disclavier, Andy brings a rare combination of skill, dedication, and professionalism.

Specializing in Player Systems

Few technicians have extensive hands-on experience with player piano technologies. Andy Ballard Piano Service is proud to offer expert installation, upgrades, and repairs for modern player systems, ensuring your piano remains a source of enjoyment for years to come.

Serving DFW and Beyond

Andy proudly serves the entire Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex, traveling to homes, schools, churches, and performance venues throughout the region. Areas covered include Dallas, Collin, Denton, Tarrant, Johnson, Ellis, and Rockwall counties — ensuring responsive, personal service where and when you need it.
