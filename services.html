Our Piano Services

Andy Ballard Piano Service offers a full range of expert services to help you protect, restore, and enjoy your piano at its highest potential. Serving Dallas, Collin, Denton, Tarrant, Johnson, Ellis, and Rockwall counties, we provide meticulous craftsmanship, personalized attention, and decades of experience you can trust.

Piano Tuning

Precision tuning ensures your piano maintains its beautiful sound. Whether for home, stage, or studio, we offer professional tuning services designed to meet the specific needs of your instrument and playing style.

Piano Repair

From sticky keys to broken pedals and action regulation, Andy provides comprehensive repairs to restore performance, reliability, and feel to your piano.

Piano Restoration

We offer partial or full restorations, including key repairs, hammer replacement, action rebuilding, voicing, and cabinet touch-ups to bring older pianos back to life with care and authenticity.

Player Piano System Service (PianoDisc & QRS)

Few piano technicians specialize in player systems. Andy Ballard Piano Service is proud to offer expert installation, troubleshooting, upgrades, and repairs for PianoDisc and QRS Pianomation systems, ensuring smooth performance and reliable enjoyment for years to come.

Additional Services

  • Humidity control system evaluation and maintenance
  • Pre-purchase piano evaluations
  • Keytop replacement and cleaning
  • Action regulation and voicing
  • String replacement and minor rebuilding
